Privacy Policy for Hashtag Generator

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information Collection and Use

The Hashtag Generator extension collects and stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • Your hashtag generation preferences and settings
  • Saved hashtag sets and categories
  • UI preferences and customizations
  • Category preferences and frequently used options

2. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We implement the following practices:

  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • All data remains on your local device
  • You can clear all stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • We don't track your browsing history or activities

3. Advertisements

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. This involves:

  • Cookies for ad personalization
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Ad performance metrics

You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings.

4. Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and hashtag sets
  • Clipboard: To copy generated hashtags
  • Active Tab: To interact with the current page

5. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension interface.

6. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or the extension, contact us at:

Email: hashtaggenerator11@gmail.com

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Disable the extension at any time
